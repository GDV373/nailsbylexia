Policies

A few simple guidelines so appointments stay smooth, calm, and on time.

Booking

Booking

  • Please use the online booking page to secure your appointment.
  • Please choose the service that best matches what you want so the right amount of time is reserved.
  • If you would like to change or add more nail art on the day, this depends on the time available.
  • If you need to reschedule, please do so with at least 48 hours notice where possible.
Cancellations

Cancellations & No Shows

  • Please cancel or reschedule at least 48 hours before your appointment whenever possible.
  • Repeated late cancellations or no-shows may affect future booking access.
  • If you are more than 15 minutes late, the appointment may need to be shortened or cancelled depending on the schedule.
Nails

Removal & Nail Fixes

  • Please arrive with clean, bare nails unless removal has been arranged in advance.
  • If you think a natural nail needs attention before your appointment, please mention it before booking.
  • If a nail fix is needed soon after your appointment because of prep or application, please get in touch so it can be reviewed.
Other

Other

  • Please arrive on time for your appointment.
  • This is a private home salon, so appointments are for one client at a time only.
  • There is a cat in the home, so please keep this in mind if you have a severe allergy.
  • Photos of your nails may be taken. If you do not want them featured, please let me know.
